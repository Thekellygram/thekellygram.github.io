About Us Koppoh is a digital platform and was born in 2017 due to the communication gap that was evident between fundraisers and investors. We realised that on one hand, businesses looking to raise funds lack the network required to raise capital and are usually not prepared for the fundraising process. On another hand, investors do not always have the network to source quality transactions and are also often not well-placed to properly assess local risk.
