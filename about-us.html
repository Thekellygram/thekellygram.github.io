MSMEs are the Engine
of Growth in Africa Koppoh seeks to empower MSMEs to play
this role.
Our Story Founded in response to the wide funding gap in Africa, Koppoh connects businesses in need of capital with investors who are looking for the right investment opportunities. Asides from making documentation easy for fundraisers, we also help investors to save time and effort by identifying high - quality deals. Whether you are a fundraiser or financier, we are always keen to help.
What we stand for
Vision
African MSMEs are empowered to thrive because of easy-to-use and affordable digital business support solutions.
Mission
To develop simple, accessible and innovative digital business support solutions that meet the evolving needs of African MSMEs.
Purpose
Playing our part in the development of Africa by providing innovative digital solutions that empower MSMEs to drive shared prosperity
