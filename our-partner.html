Partners stories
Fidelity Bank is a trusted partner for MSMEs in Nigeria, with a focus on providing tailored offerings to help these businesses build the capacity they need to succeed. As part of this commitment, Fidelity Bank has partnered with Koppoh to provide affordable loans to MSMEs. With Koppoh's expertise in digital business support solutions and Fidelity Bank's commitment to providing low-cost banking products and services, this partnership is designed to help MSMEs in Nigeria access the funding they need to grow and thrive.
